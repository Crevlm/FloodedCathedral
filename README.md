# Flooded Cathedral

> A Quake 1 single-player level focused on environmental storytelling, atmospheric exploration, and player-driven navigation.

> To play the map on your own please navigate to the **Releases** tab on the right side and download the Cathedral v1.0.0. bsp file.

## Overview

**Flooded Cathedral** is a gothic-inspired Quake level built in **TrenchBroom** using original Quake 1 assets and mapping tools.

The project focuses heavily on environmental wayfinding and readable level design without relying on objective markers or direct player instruction. Instead, the map uses lighting, architectural landmarks, verticality, environmental framing, and encounter flow to naturally guide players through the flooded cathedral ruins.

---

## Core Design Pillars

### Environmental Wayfinding
Players are guided using:
- Lighting contrast
- Sightlines
- Architectural framing
- Landmarks and vertical spaces
- Looping pathways and progression reveals

### Atmospheric Exploration
The map emphasizes:
- Gothic architecture
- Flooded ruins
- Isolation and tension
- Environmental storytelling
- Spatial immersion

### Combat & Movement
Encounters are designed around:
- Arena readability
- Movement pressure
- Vertical combat spaces
- Progression gating
- Classic Quake encounter flow

## Development Focus

This project explores:
- Quake-style spatial design
- Arena combat readability
- Environmental navigation systems
- Lighting as player guidance
- Iterative level design workflows

The level was iterated through playtesting and feedback with a focus on improving:
- Navigation clarity
- Combat pacing
- Player orientation
- Environmental readability
